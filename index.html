Responsibilities at my last job

  • Head of State
    As Head of State, I go on official State visits abroad. I also invite other world leaders to come to the United Kingdom.
  • Head of the Armed Forces
    I am the only person who can declare when the country is at war and when war is over.
  • Head of the Church of England
    I appoint archbishops and bishops on the advice of the Prime Minister.
  • Government Duties
    Every day I receive documents and reports from the government ministers and Commonwealth officials. They must all be read and signed by me.
  • Represent the Nation
    I represent the nation at times of great celebration or sorrow. For instance, I lay a wreath at Remembrance Day ceremony each year to honour the members of the armed forces who have died fighting for our country.
  • Visits
    I spend a huge amount of time travelling around the country visiting hospitals, schools, factories etc.
